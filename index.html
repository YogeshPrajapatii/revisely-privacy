Privacy Policy

Revisely  ·  Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Revisely ("we", "our", or "us") is a DSA and Coding Logic quiz app. We are committed to transparency regarding how we handle user information. This policy describes the data handled by our Android application.

2. Information We Collect

Revisely collects limited information to provide a personalized learning experience:

  • User-Provided Information: We store a Username and Avatar preference that you set within the app.
  • User-Generated Content: Personal study notes and "Logic Cards" created by you in the Notebook section are stored on your device.
  • App Activity: We track your quiz progress, XP, streaks, and levels to provide core app functionality.

We do not collect sensitive information such as your email, phone number, or precise location.

3. Cloud Sync & Data Backup

Revisely uses the Android Auto-Backup service. This means your app data (including progress, username, and notes) is automatically synced to your private Google Drive account.

  • This allows you to restore your progress when you reinstall the app or switch devices.
  • We (the developers) do not have access to this backup data; it is managed entirely by Google.
  • You can disable this feature in your Android system settings under Settings → Google → Backup.

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Identifiers: Such as the Android Advertising ID (AAID).
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with ads.

This data is used by Google to provide personalized ads and analytics. You can manage your ad preferences in your device's Google settings.

5. Notifications & Permissions

Revisely sends local notifications for reminders. To ensure these work correctly:

  • We use the RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED permission to reschedule your local reminders after a device restart.
  • No data regarding your notification schedule is transmitted to external servers.

6. Data Security & Retention

Your data is stored locally on your device and within your personal Google Cloud backup. We do not maintain any external databases. Data is retained as long as the app is installed or until you clear the app data in your system settings.
