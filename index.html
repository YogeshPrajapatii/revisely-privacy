Privacy Policy

Revisely  ·  Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Revisely ("we", "our", or "us") is a DSA and Coding Logic quiz app built for learners. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Android application.

2. Information We Collect

Revisely does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). Specifically:

  • We do not require account creation or login.
  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or location.
  • All quiz progress, XP, streaks, and settings are stored locally on your device only using Room Database and DataStore.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Revisely uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device information and use it to show personalized ads in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

  • Ad data is handled solely by Google AdMob.
  • You can opt out of personalized ads via your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.
  • Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

4. Notifications

Revisely sends optional local notifications (streak reminders, daily goal alerts). These notifications are triggered entirely on-device using WorkManager — no data is sent to any server.

You can disable notifications at any time via your device's app notification settings.

5. Third-Party Services

The only third-party SDK integrated into Revisely is Google AdMob. No analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs are included.

6. Children's Privacy

Revisely is designed for learners aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, security depends on your device's own protection mechanisms. We do not transmit any user data over the internet.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
